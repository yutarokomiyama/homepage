Yutaro Komiyama, Ph.D.

小宮山 裕太郎

Postdoctoral Researcher
Graduate School of Informatics, Chiba University

I am a researcher in the field of high-frequency power electronics. My current work focuses on developing compact and efficient power converters using GaN and SiC devices. I am particularly interested in wireless power transfer, resonant converters, and ML-based circuit designs. I received my Ph.D. in Engineering from Chiba University, Japan, in March 2025. Please feel free to get in touch if you have any opportunities or would like to connect.
